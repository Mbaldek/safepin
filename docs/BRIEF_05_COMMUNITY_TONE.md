## Tone principles for future copy

1. **Lead with community, not danger.** "Votre quartier veille" not "Danger signalé"
2. **Use "nous/ensemble" language.** Collective, not individual fear
3. **Emojis are your friend.** 💛 🏘️ 🤝 — they soften without losing meaning
4. **SOS is the exception.** When it's truly emergency, be direct and clear. No emojis in the SOS flow.
5. **Empty states are opportunities.** They're the first thing new users see. Make them inviting, not clinical.
